Mayo MMA & Kickboxing Academy

Empowering individuals through elite training in MMA, Muay Thai, BJJ, Boxing, and Kali at Mayo Academy, we pride ourselves on delivering world-class martial arts training tailored to kids and adults alike.

Whether you’re looking to boost your fitness, master self-defense, or pursue a competitive fighting career, we provide a safe and encouraging environment to help you achieve your goals.

Why Choose Mayo MMA Academy


  • Expert Coaches: Our experienced instructors bring decades of experience in professional fighting and coaching to every class.
  • Tailored Training: We adapt our programs to meet the needs of each student, whether you’re a beginner or an advanced fighter.
  • Community Focus: At Mayo Academy, you’re not just joining a gym; you’re becoming part of a supportive and motivating family.
  • World-Class Facilities: Our academy is equipped with the latest gear and a clean, spacious training environment.

Our Amazing Classes


Are you ready to challenge yourself, grow stronger, and join a community that supports your goals? Whether you’re a parent seeking a constructive activity for your child or an adult aiming to refine your skills, Mayo MMA Academy is the place for you.


Kids MMA Training

For ages 6-14
Our Kids MMA program is designed to instill confidence, discipline, and resilience. Your child will learn a combination of striking, grappling, and self-defense techniques while improving physical fitness and mental focus. Our instructors prioritize safety and fun, ensuring your child develops skills and friendships that last a lifetime.

Muay Thai (KickBoxing)

For beginners to advanced practitioners
Known as “The Art of Eight Limbs,” Muay Thai combines punches, kicks, elbows, and knees for a full-body workout and combat sport. Our classes emphasize proper technique, power, and strategy, whether you’re training for fitness, self-defense, or competition.

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ)

For all skill levels
Learn the art of ground fighting and submission grappling with our Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu program. Ideal for self-defense and competitive practice, BJJ is a discipline that builds problem-solving skills, strength, and perseverance. Our classes focus on technique and control, making it a perfect sport for anyone.

Boxing

Master the Sweet Science
Our boxing classes focus on developing speed, precision, and power. Whether you’re an aspiring fighter or just looking to improve your conditioning, you’ll gain skills that enhance both physical and mental agility. Led by expert coaches, our sessions cater to all experience levels.

Doce Pares Eskrima (FMA)

A weapons based fighting system
Discover the rich traditions of Filipino combat systems, including stick and knife techniques, close-quarter combat, and empty-hand defense. Enhance your agility, coordination, and reflexes.
