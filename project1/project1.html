About Mayo Academy


Empowering individuals through elite training in MMA, Muay Thai (KickBoxing), BJJ, Boxing, and Doce Pares Eskrima (Filipino Martial Arts) at Mayo Academy, we pride ourselves on delivering world-class martial arts training tailored to kids and adults alike.

Whether you’re looking to boost your fitness, master self-defense, or pursue a competitive fighting career, we provide a safe and encouraging environment to help you achieve your goals.


Why Choose Mayo Academy


  • Expert coaches:
    Our experienced instructors bring decades of experience in professional fighting and coaching to every class.
  • Tailored training:
    We adapt our programs to meet the needs of each student, whether you’re a beginner or an advanced fighter.
  • Community focus:
    At Mayo Academy, you’re not just joining a gym; you’re becoming part of a supportive and motivating family.
  • World-class facilities:
    Our academy is equipped with the latest gear and a clean, spacious training environment.

Our Amazing Classes


Are you ready to challenge yourself, grow stronger, and join a community that supports your goals? Whether you’re a parent seeking a constructive activity for your child or an adult aiming to refine your skills, Mayo Academy is the place for you.




Kids MMA Training

For ages 6-14
Your child will learn a combination of striking, grappling, and self-defense techniques while improving physical fitness and mental focus.

Muay Thai (KickBoxing)

For beginners to advanced
Known as “The Art of Eight Limbs,” Muay Thai combines punches, kicks, elbows, and knees for a full-body workout and combat sport.

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ)

For all skill levels
Focusing on ground fighting and submission, BJJ is a discipline that builds problem-solving skills, strength, and perseverance.

Boxing

Master the Sweet Science
Our boxing classes focus on developing speed, precision, and power, catering to all experience levels.

Doce Pares Eskrima (FMA)

A weapons based fighting system
Discover the rich traditions of Filipino combat systems, including stick and knife techniques, close-quarter combat, and empty-hand defense.

Self-Defense for Real-World Situations

An unarmed tactics course
This practical course covers basic and advanced striking techniques, grappling and escapes from common holds, situational awareness and de-escalation tactics.
